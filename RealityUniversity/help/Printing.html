Printing: Having the Results on Hand

After the data entry and processing is finished, all that is
left to do is printing out the processed student surveys to be
prepared for the RealityU session.

Process for printing individual surveys:

  • When you press the magnifying glass next to student name,
    the Survey Form pops out.
  • Click the 'Print' button at the bottom of the form.
  • Two other forms pop up: a print preview and a printing dialog box.
    The print preview shows what the printout will look like.
  • The printing dialog box shows what printer you are connected to,
    the print range and number of copies you can make(if need be).
    Once all that has been sorted out, click 'OK' to prcoeed with
    the printing of individual surveys.
