Data Entry Procedure

Data entry is done entirely on the survey form page, as shown above,
regardless of whether you are working on an existing group or a new group.
Here's how it done:

  • Fill out all textboxes, radio buttons and scroll-down choices as one goes through
    the written survey form.
  • If you miss any textboxes, an error message will pop up and tell to fill it out.
  • If you make a mistake, simply hit the Clear button and start over.
  • Once you are done, click Add Button and the student's name will pop up
    on the rightside panel which will show you the students added to your group.
  • Note: You have options to view, edit and delete any student(s) from the rightside panel.
  • The top right side panel shows the group name, no. of students in the group
    and creation/last modified dates.
  • Before printing, click the 'Process' button to process allthe students in your group.
  • Once you are done with the whole group, click Print Survey button to get a printout of survey(s).
